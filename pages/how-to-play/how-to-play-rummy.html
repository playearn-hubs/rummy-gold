How to Play Rummy — Complete Beginner Guide

Learn rummy from scratch: objective, sequences, sets, and how to declare on Rummy Gold.

Rummy basics

The goal in 13-card Indian rummy is to arrange all cards into valid sequences and sets, then declare. You need at least one pure sequence (without joker).

Steps to play

  1. Each player gets 13 cards
  2. Draw and discard each turn
  3. Form sequences (3+ consecutive cards same suit) and sets (3–4 same rank)
  4. Declare when all cards are valid

What is a pure sequence in rummy?

A sequence of 3+ consecutive cards of the same suit without a joker — mandatory to declare.
